View filter
Changing this instantly filters personas, products, assets, templates, and brands in GS4PM.
Tagging
When tagging is enabled, click any card/tile in GS4PM to add or remove its tag for the selected customer.
Customers
Customers are stored in Chrome sync so you can reuse them across GS4PM sections.
Tip: You can also right-click any tile, asset, template, persona, product, or dropdown option in GS4PM and choose “Tag element for current customer”.
